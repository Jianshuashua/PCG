# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in relation to the PCG HDA Toolset.

### 1. Data Collection & Hardware Binding
Although our software is bound to specific computer hardware to prevent unauthorized distribution, we **do not collect or store** any personal identifiable information (PII) from your system. The "Machine ID" generated is a unique cryptographic hash used solely for license activation.

### 2. Internet Usage & Connectivity
Our software requires an internet connection to function. However, this connection is used **strictly for time-check verification** to validate subscription or trial periods. 
* We do not track your location.
* We do not monitor your project data or workflow.
* No other background data transmission occurs during the process.

### 3. Data Sharing with Third Parties
We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding data selling. We **never** sell, trade, or rent your personal information, email address, or hardware identifiers to any third parties.

### 4. Payment Security
All financial transactions are handled securely by our payment processor, **Paddle**. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card numbers or banking details.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the email address provided on our official GitHub page.
